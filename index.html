We at Falafilo prepare these oriental recipes with their healthy ingredients And its natural spices with a lot of love to make it special and complete the story of oriental meals in Russia that you must definitely try

Aleppo spices are natural and healthy, away from artificial flavors, flavor enhancers and preservatives Which scientific research has proven that its continuous consumption leads to major health risks that may not appear immediately.

We at Falafello put health first along with distinctive flavor Therefore, we use chicken and sheep meat (halal) with special natural spices , which are the strongest in terms of flavor and nutritional value to provide the body with its needs for a day full of energy

Aleppo cuisine is considered one of the most famous international kitchens, historically and culturally, even today, where many types of foods became famous and spread to the whole world. Such as falafel, shawarma, kebab grilled on charcoal, and a lot of appetizers and desserts specializing in pistachios, known globally as Aleppo pistachios.
